# Privacy Policy

Zvaka stores your profile, meal history, coach chats, saved photos, and on-device AI model choices locally on your device by default.

If you enable optional sync features:

- Google Drive backup stores your app data in your private app data area in Google Drive.
- Health Connect calorie sync writes saved meal calorie totals to Health Connect as nutrition records.
- Model improvement sharing uploads a downscaled meal photo, detected meal description, and calorie estimate when that feature is enabled.

Zvaka does not sell personal data.

Health Connect data access is only used for the features you explicitly enable.
